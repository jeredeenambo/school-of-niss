EDUCATION DEPARTMENT

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders

Led by Miss Zarina Bakari, the Education Department at the NYS Institute of Social Studies is committed to providing high-quality education in early childhood development and child care. With a focus on nurturing young minds and fostering inclusive learning environments, the department plays a vital role in shaping the future of education.

Diploma in ECDE and Child Care Courses

Our diploma programs in Early Childhood Development and Child Care emphasize the importance of holistic development and personalized learning. Through hands-on experience and theoretical foundations, students learn to create nurturing environments that support children's growth and well-being.
